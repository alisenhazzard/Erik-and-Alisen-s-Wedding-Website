Flying

The closest airport is in Jamestown, New York, but it is usually cheaper to fly into Buffalo and then drive to Jamestown. The drive is about an hour and a half. (If you would like to share a rental car with another wedding guest, feel free to leave a note in the guest book.)

Hotels

We have a group-rate at the Comfort Inn, located at 2800 North Main St. Jamestown (call 716-664-5920 and ask for the Warner/Hazzard Wedding Rate). There are a few other hotels available nearby, including the Hampton Inn (4 West Oak Hill Road) and a Best Western Plus in Downtown Jamestown.

Things to do in the Jamestown area:

Chautauqua/Lake Erie Wine Trail - This is a great part of the country for wine-lovers, so be sure to check out some of the wineries or take the tour!
Lucille Ball and Desi Arnaz Center - Lucille Ball was originally from Jamestown, and the center is located in Downtown Jamestown. It has the Lucy-Desi Museum and the Desilu Playhouse. It's only $15 for admission to both, and a must-see for Lucy fans.
Chautauqua Area - There are many things to do around Chautauqua Lake. The Chautauqua Institution will be closed for the season, but visitors are still welcome to browse the area and enjoy the walk through the beautiful residences. You can pass by the historic Anatheum Hotel and end up on Chautauqua Lake for a boat ride. There are also art galleries, farmers markets, and museums around the lake.
Ellicotville The Ellicottville Brewery, fun shops, and lots of activities for the outdoorsy-types. About 50 minutes east of Jamestown.
Fenton History Center in Jamestown A fascinating history museum about Jamestown and the Chautauqua area.
Peek n' Peak Resort and Spa - Located only 30 minutes west of Jamestown, the resort features two golf courses, a luxury day spa, and an indoor swimming pool. Located about 45 minutes west of Jamestown.
